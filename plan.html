Site Plan for Adam & Eve Clothings

Site Name

Adam & Eve Clothings was chosen to reflect the concept of starting anew with sustainable fashion choices.

Site Purpose

The purpose of Adam & Eve Clothings is to educate and promote sustainable fashion choices through showcasing eco-friendly clothing options, brand stories emphasizing sustainability practices, and providing tips on eco-friendly fashion choices.

Scenarios

  • What are the latest trends in sustainable fashion?
  • How can I find organic cotton apparel for kids?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #0066cc (used for headings)
Secondary Color: #009933 (used for accents)

Typography

Main Font: Arial, sans-serif (used for body text)
Heading Font: Arial, sans-serif (used for headings)
